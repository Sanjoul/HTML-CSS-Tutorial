About Gurans Hydro

Overview of Gurans
Hydro's involvement in
hydropower projects:

Gurans Hydro, a subsidiary of SG Group, is dedicated to harnessing Nepal's abundant hydropower potential to drive economic growth and environmental sustainability. With a focus on innovation and excellence, we are committed to delivering reliable and renewable energy solutions to power communities across the country.

“Our mission at Gurans Hydro is to lead the way in sustainable energy development, empowering Nepal with clean, reliable, and affordable electricity. We envision a future where hydropower plays a central role in driving progress, prosperity, and environmental stewardship.”
